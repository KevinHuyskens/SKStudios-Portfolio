Supernova

Game dev

Supernova was one of the largest tech events in Europe. They asked us to develop a VR game for it. We decided on creating a VR space shooter. it involed multiple waves of enemies and at the end a big boss. What the player didn't know at the start tho, was that there was a second player with an iPad helping him. The player with the iPad could view the space in AR. So in essence the game was an AR/VR multiplayer experience. It was a huge success at Supernova.
